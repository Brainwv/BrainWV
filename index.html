About Us

BrainWV

Brainwv was founded in 2025 by Miss Nishu and Mr. Prince Jha while they were third-year B.Tech students in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. Their expertise led to a successful inaugural project at Subros Private Limited, where they resolved issues with a belt conveyor and airflow machine using PLC programming. Within the first month, Brainwave completed projects for three major companies—Subros, Mahindra, and Falcon Autotech—demonstrating its efficiency and commitment to high-quality automation solutions.



Our Mission

Brainwv is committed to creating value for customers, employees, and society by delivering high-quality products and services. We prioritize trust, innovation, and excellence to help our clients achieve their business goals.



Our Vision

Brainwv aims to be a leading force in automation and technology, delivering high-quality solutions that drive business success and innovation.



Our Services

  • Automation Solution

    At BrainWV, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge automation solutions to streamline your workflows, enhance efficiency, and drive business growth. Our expertise spans across industries, providing customized AI-driven automation, robotic process automation (RPA), industrial automation, and smart software solutions tailored to your needs.

  • Conveyor Installation

    At BrainWV, we specialize in conveyor installation solutions that streamline material handling, improve workflow efficiency, and optimize industrial operations. Our team of experts ensures seamless installation, maintenance, and customization to meet your specific business needs.

  • HMI Installation

    At BrainWV, we specialize in Human-Machine Interface (HMI) installation services, delivering user-friendly control solutions that optimize industrial operations. Our expertise ensures seamless integration, precise configuration, and enhanced automation to streamline your processes.

  • CNC Drilling Machine

    At BrainWV, we provide state-of-the-art CNC Drilling Machine solutions that ensure high precision, efficiency, and productivity for industrial applications. Our cutting-edge machines are designed for automated drilling, superior accuracy, and enhanced operational control.

  • Repairing

    At BrainWV, we specialize in comprehensive repairing services to restore and enhance the performance of your industrial equipment and machinery. Our skilled technicians provide quick diagnostics, precision repairs, and preventive maintenance to minimize downtime and ensure optimal functionality.
